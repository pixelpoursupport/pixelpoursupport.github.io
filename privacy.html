Privacy Policy — §1

Information Collection and Use

Pixel Pour is a free-to-play mobile game supported by advertising. The app collects a limited amount of anonymous data to support the global leaderboard feature, including a unique device identifier, an optional username chosen by the user, and the player's current level. This data is stored securely on Supabase servers located in the European Union.

The app also uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you, such as Advertising IDs, as well as anonymous usage and analytics data (such as app events, session duration, and crash reports), in order to serve relevant advertisements and improve the application experience. These services operate under their own privacy policies.

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob by Google
  • Firebase (Google LLC)
  • Google Analytics for Firebase
  • Supabase

Privacy Policy — §2

Data Retention

Game progress (such as the current level) is stored both locally on your device and on Supabase servers to support the global leaderboard. If you choose to set a username, it will be stored permanently on Supabase servers and cannot be changed after it has been set. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us at pixelpour.support@gmail.com.

Firebase Analytics may collect and retain aggregated, anonymised usage data on Google's servers. By default, Google Analytics for Firebase retains event-level data for 2 months and user-level data for up to 14 months. This data is governed by Google's own retention and deletion policies.

The unique device identifier used for the leaderboard is stored on Supabase servers and is retained for as long as the player's account exists. It is never shared with third parties and is used solely to identify the player's progress across sessions and reinstalls.

Privacy Policy — §3

Children's Privacy

This application is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

Advertising within the app is configured to comply with applicable regulations regarding child-directed content, including the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and relevant Google Play policies. Firebase Analytics is used in a manner consistent with these requirements.

Privacy Policy — §4

Account Deletion

You may request the deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us via email. Once deleted, your username, device identifier, and level data will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

How to request account deletion Send an email to pixelpour.support@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request". Please include your username (if you have set one) in the email. We will process your request and confirm deletion within 30 days.

Please note that deleting your account will permanently remove your leaderboard position and game progress stored on our servers. Progress stored locally on your device will not be affected and can be cleared manually through your device settings.
